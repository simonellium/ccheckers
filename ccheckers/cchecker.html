Background

I wrote this game as a port of the Chinese Checker Solitaire game my fiancee plays in real life. There was a lack of football on TV one Saturday afternoon, so I wrote this while she flipped through bridal gown magazines.

The game is written in javascript, powered by the Raphael SVG library.

The tabular layout is stolen from an elated article.

Rules:

Click on a marble to pick it up. Click on a slot to put it down. However, the marble may only be put down if it jumps over exactly one other marble and no empty slots in between. Jumping one marble over another removes the marble you jumped over from board. Your objective: end up with as few marbles as possible.

(According to this there is always a one-marble solution, but that board has a different layout, so the proof may not be applicable to this one.)
